Baking Tips

General Baking Tips:

1. All ovens may cook slightly different. If baking with convection reduce temperature to 325F.

2. Place oven rack in center of oven.

3. Cool on rack after baking.

Baking Instructions

Cookies:
Chocolate Chunk/Chocolate Chunk with Walnuts

Makes 18 cookies

1. Pre-heat

Pre-heat oven to 350 degrees (150 degrees C).

2. Prepare

On a cookie sheet lined with parchment paper, or non-stick pan, roll dough in balls spacing 1 ½ inches apart.

3. Bake

Bake 14-15 minutes until cookie edges are brown. For a crispier cookie bake 15-16 minutes.

Cakes:
1. Pre-heat

Pre-heat oven to 350 F degrees (150 degrees C). Make sure rack is in the center of the oven.

2. Spray

Spray the pan with non-stick cooking/baking spray.

3. Prepare

Scoop batter into pan 2/3’s full.

4. Bake

Bake 15-25 minutes and let cool.

Each container bakes the following.

8-inch cake pan bake for 24 minutes or until toothpick comes out dry.

12 cupcakes bake for 15 minutes or until toothpick comes out dry.

6 individual cakes - bake for 18 minutes or until toothpick comes out dry.

Brownies:
Blondies and Chocolate Blondies
1. Pre-heat

Pre-heat oven to 350 F degrees (150 degrees C).

2. Spray/Spread

Grease an 8-inch square baking pan using butter or nonstick spray.

3. Prepare

Let batter come to room temperature. Pour or scoop batter in pan spreading evenly.

4. Bake

Bake 25-30 minutes until center is barely firm in the middle.

Gluten-free Brownies
Gluten Free Peanut Butter Brownies
1. Pre-heat

Pre-heat oven to 350 F degrees (150 degrees C).

2. Spray/Spread

Grease an 8-inch square baking pan using butter or nonstick spray.

3. Prepare

Let batter come to room temperature. Pour or scoop batter in pan spreading evenly.

4. Bake

Bake 25-30 minutes.
